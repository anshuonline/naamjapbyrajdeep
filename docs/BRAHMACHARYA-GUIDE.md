## üìã How It Works

### 1. Daily Check-in
- **When**: Every day before 12:00 AM midnight
- **How**: Click the "Today's Check-in" button
- **Frequency**: Once per day only
- **Rule**: Must check-in daily to maintain streak

### 2. Streak System
- **Streak Builds**: Check-in daily before midnight
- **Streak Breaks**: Missing a single day resets streak to 0
- **Current Streak**: Shows your active consecutive days
- **Best Streak**: Records your longest streak ever

### 3. Goal Completion
- Each goal is unlocked sequentially
- When you reach a goal (e.g., 7 days), it gets marked complete ‚úÖ
- The next goal becomes active üéØ
- Locked goals üîí appear grayed out

### 4. Progress Tracking
- **Current Streak**: Your ongoing streak count
- **Current Level**: Level 1, 2, or 3+
- **Next Goal**: Your upcoming milestone
- **Total Check-ins**: Total times you've checked in
- **Best Streak**: Your personal record
- **Goals Completed**: Number of goals achieved
- **Days Since Start**: Total days since first check-in
